Pumpkin Soup

INGREDIENTS

  • Hokkaido pumpkin: 1 kg
  • Onion: 1/2 piece
  • Garlic: 1-2 cloves
  • Ginger: 1-2 cm
  • Broth: 500 ml
  • Coconut milk: 200 ml
  • Lemon juice: 2-3 tablespoons
  • Nutmeg: 1/3 teaspoon
  • Salt and pepper: to taste

DIRECTIONS:

  1. Peel and clean the pumpkin, removing any inedible parts, and bake it until soft (about 25 minutes at 180 degrees Celsius).
  2. Place the chopped onion, garlic, ginger, roasted pumpkin, pour in the broth, coconut milk (homemade or canned), lemon juice, and season to taste in the blender jar.
  3. Blend using the "SOUP" function in a Wartmann blender, which will cook the soup. If you don't have this function, blend the ingredients for 1-2 minutes, and after that, transfer the contents of the blender jar to a pot and simmer for a few minutes.
  4. Serve with a dollop of plant-based cream and pumpkin seeds.
